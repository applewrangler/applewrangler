Frequently Asked Questions

How does the selling process work?
Get an instant quote using our custom quote tool. If you're happy with the offer, ship your device to us (we provide a free shipping label). Once we inspect the device, we'll send your payment promptly.
What devices do you accept?
We accept a wide range of Apple devices, including iPhones, iPads, MacBooks, Apple Vision Pro, and more. Check our buyback widget for eligible devices.
How long does the process take?
We typically process devices within 24 hours of receipt. Payment is sent immediately after processing.
How will I be paid?
We offer payment via PayPal or check. You can select your preferred method during the selling process.
